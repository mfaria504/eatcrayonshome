Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 8, 2026

Rise Marketing Co., LLC d/b/a EatCrayons ("EatCrayons," "we," "us," or "our") operates the website www.eatcrayons.com (the "Website"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you visit or interact with our Website. By using the Website, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us and certain information that is collected automatically when you visit the Website.

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

We only ask for what we need to do our work. When you use our forms and tools, we collect the information you voluntarily share with us.

ICP Matchmaker Survey (eatcrayons.com/match)

The Matchmaker is a ten-question diagnostic that helps us, and you, figure out whether we are the right growth partner for your business. You can take it anonymously: no name, email, or phone number is required to see your result. What we do collect are your answers, which include:

  • Your approximate annual revenue range
  • Whether you sell to businesses, consumers, or both
  • Your typical deal size
  • The current state of your marketing function
  • How much untapped growth potential you see ahead of you
  • How you think about ROI, patience, and long-term investment
  • Your leadership style and how involved you stay in execution
  • Your standing and authority in your industry
  • Who actually makes the buying decision in your deals
  • The monthly investment range that feels right for a real engagement

From those answers we calculate a match score, a tier, and a short set of signals that summarize your profile. None of this information is personally identifying on its own.

Contact Form

When you reach out to start a conversation with us, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your business name
  • The services you are interested in (Strategy, Infrastructure, Delivery, or a full Growth Engine)
  • A short message describing your situation: what is working, what is broken, and what you are trying to build next

If you completed the Matchmaker before reaching the contact form, your Matchmaker results are submitted alongside your message so we can arrive at our first conversation already knowing the context.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit the Website, we automatically collect certain information through HubSpot tracking technology, which may include:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent on pages, and navigation patterns
  • Referring website or source
  • Device type and screen resolution
  • Cookies and similar tracking identifiers

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To evaluate potential client fit through the ICP Matchmaker survey
  • To respond to inquiries submitted through the contact form
  • To understand how visitors interact with our Website and improve its content and functionality
  • To communicate with you about our services, if you have contacted us or submitted information
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms and Conditions

3. Data Storage

Personal information collected through the ICP Matchmaker survey and the contact form is stored in Supabase, a hosted database platform. We also use HubSpot for website analytics and visitor tracking. We implement commercially reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

We may disclose your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share information with third-party service providers (such as Supabase and HubSpot) that assist us in operating the Website and storing data, subject to their own privacy policies and contractual obligations to protect your data.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required to do so by law, court order, or governmental regulation, or if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, the safety of others, or to investigate fraud.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of assets, your personal information may be transferred as part of that transaction, and you will be notified via a prominent notice on our Website.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies (via HubSpot) to collect and store information about your interactions with the Website. Cookies are small data files placed on your device that help us analyze web traffic and improve your experience.

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain features of the Website.
