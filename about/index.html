Coloring Outside the Lines

In 2020, amidst the chaos of a global pandemic, Jess and I founded EatCrayons, driven by our vision for more impactful and satisfying work. Despite starting with significant hurdles, including minimal funds and no initial offerings or clientele, our entrepreneurial spirit never wavered. Okay fine, maybe it did slightly but we recovered.

What does this mean for you? Our adventure led to a profound discovery: we can deliver exceptional value where larger agencies repeatedly fall short. This isn't just a success story, it's a promise to you. EatCrayons has evolved into a dynamic, efficient powerhouse, uniquely positioned to spur revenue growth and secure new opportunities for your business.

Choosing EatCrayons means partnering with a team that's not only overcome our own set of challenges but is also deeply committed to ensuring your company doesn't just survive but thrives, cutting through the empty promises to deliver tangible results.

Matthew Faria President & Cofounder
