The format

One hour.
Structured to produce.

10 min

Opening round

Each member states what's live for them this week in 60–90 seconds. No backstory, no context-setting. Just the current situation. This surfaces the raw material and sets the stakes for the session.

5 min

Thread selection

The room identifies which two or three threads are most generative. Done collaboratively, not by the host. The room owns its agenda.

35 min

Deep work

The selected threads get full attention. This is not a presentation format. It is a dialogue. The goal: leave the person who surfaced the problem with at least one concrete thing they didn't have before.

5 min

Closing round

Each member states one thing they're committing to before next week. One sentence. This creates the accountability loop that makes the weekly cadence meaningful.
