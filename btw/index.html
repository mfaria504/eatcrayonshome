How it works

One hour. Every week.
No fluff.

10 min

Opening round

Each member shares what's live for them this week. 60 seconds. No backstory. Just the current situation.

5 min

Thread selection

The room picks which threads to pull. Collaboratively, not by the host. The room owns its agenda.

35-40 min

Deep work

Selected threads get full attention. Not presentations — dialogue. Leave with at least one thing you didn't have before.

5 min

Closing round

One sentence. What you're committing to before next week. That's the accountability loop.

The room

Four things we don't
compromise on.

01

Practitioners only

Everyone in the room is operating something real. The currency is direct experience — what you've tried, measured, and learned.

02

The present tense

"This week" is load-bearing language. What are you actually doing right now? Not theory. Not case studies from five years ago.

03

Compounding trust

The value compounds over months — shared context, pattern recognition, relationships that deepen with every session. Weekly cadence is non-negotiable.

04

Earned exclusivity

The price of admission is quality — the willingness to show up, contribute, and make the room better by being in it.
